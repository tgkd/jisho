Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 12, 2025

Overview

Jisho is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Jisho is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Local Data: Dictionary data, search history, and user preferences are stored locally on your device
  • AI Features: When using AI-powered explanations, text may be sent to AI providers (Apple Intelligence or external APIs based on your settings)
  • No Account Required: The app does not require user registration or collect personal identification information

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Provide dictionary lookup functionality
  • Save your search history locally
  • Generate AI-powered explanations when requested
  • Remember your app settings and preferences

Data Storage

All dictionary data and user preferences are stored locally on your device using SQLite and MMKV storage. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to external servers except when explicitly using AI features.

Third-Party Services

When using AI features, your queries may be processed by:

  • Apple Intelligence (local on-device processing)
  • External AI providers (when cloud AI is selected in settings)

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. All data is stored locally on your device with standard mobile operating system protections.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your search history at any time through the app settings
  • Choose which AI provider to use or disable AI features entirely
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app store or our support channels.
